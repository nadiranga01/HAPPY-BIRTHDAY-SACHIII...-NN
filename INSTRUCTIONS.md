# 🎉 Happy Birthday Sachi - Website Setup Guide

Machn, your premium birthday website is ready! It's built with HTML, CSS (Tailwind), and Vanilla JavaScript as you requested.

## 📂 How to add your Photos and Videos

To make the website yours, follow these simple steps:

1.  **Create Folders**:
    *   Create a folder named `images` in the main directory.
    *   Create a folder named `videos` in the main directory.

2.  **Add Your Photos**:
    *   Put your girlfriend's photos in the `images` folder.
    *   In `index.html` and `gallery.html`, search for `https://images.unsplash.com/...` and replace them with your local paths (e.g., `images/your_photo.jpg`).
    *   For the **Heart Animation** in `gallery.html`, add your image paths to the `photos` array at the bottom of the file.

3.  **Add Your Videos**:
    *   Put your videos in the `videos` folder.
    *   In `videos.html`, find the `<video>` tags and update the `<source src="">` with your video paths (e.g., `videos/my_video.mp4`).

4.  **Add Music**:
    *   Change the music link in `index.html`'s `<audio>` tag to your favorite romantic song.
